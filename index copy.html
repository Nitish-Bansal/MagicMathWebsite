About

Magic Math is determined to spread the beauty of contest math among young students and offer free educational resources to get started. The organization was founded in California during the COVID-19 pandemic to keep students engaged and excited about math while schools had been shut down. Since then, the organization has expanded to four different countries, educating over one hundred students.

Participating in Magic Math contests are a fun and easy way to get exposer about contest math. We help students prepare for their journey by creating contests and resources to learn from. Our prior contests along with additional resources can be found under the Resources tab.

The Program

Magic Math organizes a series of math competitions that take place every week. Anyone can register by clicking the Register tab. During the contest, students solve a variety of problems based on their grade level and recieve a rank after the contest is finished. Additionally, they get the test paper along with a Youtube video containing solutions for every problem so that they can learn from their mistakes!

Who am I?

I am Nitish Bansal, founder of Magic Math. In middle school, I participated in math competitions and turns out that was the best decision I ever made! I created Magic Math to help students develop their passion for math and motivate them to learn more. I started off teaching math, free of charge, in my community library and make Youtube videos containing interesting math tricks. Furthermore, I am an AIME qualifier, Miller MATHCOUNTS Coach, and achieved AMC8 Distinguished Honor Roll in middle school.
