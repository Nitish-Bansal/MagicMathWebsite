Instructions

Please begin the time once the test link is opened and after 45 minutes of starting, submit the test. All answers are integers. This means do not include units or decimal. There are three levels of achievement for this competition and each question is worth 1 point. Platinum is 17-20 points. Gold is 13-16 points. Silver is 9-12 points.
